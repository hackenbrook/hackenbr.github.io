Identify Copyright Restrictions

Prior to hosting webpages on the internet, we need to understand copyright restrictions.
Watch the folowing videos below and consider the following questions. You do not need to submit the answers, but there will be a final assignment related to the questions.



  • What are three types of copyright that applies to this Crash Course Video?
  • What are two ways that companies can enforce their copyright?
  • How would you define Intellectual Property using English...not legal-ese?
  • What are the three main branches of IP and Copyright?




  • How do cover songs on YouTube get around copyright rules?
  • What is a fair use exception?
  • What are three examples of where you see fair use each day?
  • What is the Copyright Golden Rule?




  • Why would you want to use a Creative Commons Licence?
  • What are three types of Creative Commons Licence?
